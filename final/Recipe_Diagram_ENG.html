Ingame Recipe Rules

  • Fixed Slots: Every recipe must consist of exactly 3 slots (3 sets of Item ×N).
  • Two-Ingredient Adjustment: If a recipe only has 2 ingredients (A×m + B×n), you must split one ingredient into two separate slots to reach a total of 3:
    • A×m + B×n → A×a + B×n + A×(m-a) (where 1 ≤ a < m)
  • Splitting Priority: Prioritize splitting the ingredient with the larger quantity or the one that is easier to divide.

Examples

  • Sweet Grapeball:
    🍇 Grape ×6 + 🍬 Sugar ×3 + 🥐 Flour ×6 — 3 distinct slots ✓
  • Oil Blend:
    🛢️ Bean Oil ×3 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 → 🛢️ Bean Oil ×2 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 + 🛢️ Bean Oil ×1
  • Glass Vial Lv2:
    🧪 Vial Lv1 ×3 → 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 + 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 + 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1
